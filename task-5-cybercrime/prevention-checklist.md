# &#x20;***🛡️ Stay Safe Online – Checklist for College Students (India)***



&#x20;**✅ Essential Do’s**



&#x20;**✔️ Use strong, unique passwords for each account (combine letters, numbers, symbols)**

&#x20;**✔️ Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on email, social media, and banking apps**

&#x20;**✔️ Verify links before clicking—hover over URLs and avoid unknown or shortened links**

&#x20;**✔️ Download apps only from trusted sources like official app stores**

&#x20;**✔️ Keep your devices updated with the latest software and antivirus protection**

&#x20;**✔️ Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive activities like banking or logging into important accounts**



**---**



&#x20;**💳 UPI \& Financial Safety (Important)**



&#x20;**✔️ Never share OTP, PIN, or UPI credentials with anyone—even if they claim to be from the bank**

&#x20;**✔️ Do not approve unknown “Collect Request” (UPI requests)—you only enter PIN to pay, not to receive money**

&#x20;**✔️ Double-check payment details (name/UPI ID) before sending money**



**---**

&#x20;**⚠️ Be Alert**



&#x20;**✔️ Do not share personal information (Aadhaar, PAN, address) on social media**

&#x20;**✔️ Beware of job/internship scams asking for registration or security fees**

&#x20;**✔️Log out from shared or public devices after use**



**---**



&#x20;**🚨 In Case of Cybercrime**



&#x20;**📢 Report immediately on: \[https://cybercrime.gov.in](https://cybercrime.gov.in)**

&#x20;**📞 Call Helpline: 1930 (for financial fraud assistance)**



**---**



**✅ Remember: Stay alert, think before you click, and act quickly if something seems suspicious.**
