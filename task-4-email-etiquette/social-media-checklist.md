## **A simple checklist with 5 Do’s and 5 Don’ts for responsible social media use as a college student**

**✅ Do’s**

**✔️ Think before posting—ensure your content is appropriate and respectful**

**✔️ Protect your privacy by adjusting security settings**

**✔️ Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation**

**✔️ Maintain a professional tone, especially on platforms like LinkedIn**

**✔️ Respect others’ opinions and engage in positive discussions**

**❌ Don’ts**

**❌ Don’t share confidential or personal information publicly**

**❌ Don’t post offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content**

**❌ Don’t overuse hashtags or spam posts**

**❌ Don’t tag people without their permission**

**❌ Don’t engage in arguments or negative online behavior**
